    Our Free-Play Model

    CCFC Academy is committed to a fully-funded, no pay-to-play philosophy. This means all costs associated with player development, including coaching, travel, equipment, and competition, are covered by the academy. We believe financial barriers should never prevent talented young athletes from reaching their full potential in soccer.
