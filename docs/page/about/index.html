Our Mission: Talent Without Barriers

CCFC Academy is committed to identifying and developing top soccer talent regardless of financial background. As a fully funded program, we eliminate the pay-to-play barrier, ensuring every gifted young athlete has the chance to reach their full potential. Operated under the Carolina Core Soccer Foundation, in proud partnership with Goal 13 Foundation and Pennybyrn, we provide an unparalleled development pathway.

Our commitment means all player expenses – from coaching and facilities to travel and gear – are covered, allowing players and families to focus solely on development and growth.

Our Story: A Vision for Carolina Soccer

CCFC Academy was founded on a simple yet powerful premise: to give every young player in the Carolinas a genuine chance to compete at the highest level of youth soccer. Recognizing the significant financial hurdles often associated with elite sports, our founders envisioned a program where talent, dedication, and passion were the only prerequisites for entry.

We began with the belief that a fully funded, merit-based academy could unlock the immense, untapped potential within our community. By partnering with dedicated foundations and organizations, we transformed this vision into reality, establishing a pathway that leads promising young athletes from local fields to the national stage of MLS NEXT, and beyond.
