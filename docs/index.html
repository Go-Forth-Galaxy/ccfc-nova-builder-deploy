Our Mission

Developing Tomorrow's Stars, Today

The Carolina Core FC Academy is a fully-funded MLS NEXT youth development program committed to identifying and nurturing top soccer talent in the High Point, NC region. Through the generous support of the Carolina Core Soccer Foundation, we provide a world-class training environment completely free of charge, removing financial barriers to elite athletic development.

Our academy focuses on holistic player development, combining rigorous on-field training with academic support and character building. We believe every aspiring athlete deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential, regardless of socioeconomic background.
